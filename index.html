About AgriDetect

AgriDetect is an innovative plant disease detection system powered by Artificial Intelligence (AI). We help farmers identify plant diseases quickly and accurately by analyzing leaf images. Our system enables farmers to take timely action, reducing crop losses and enhancing agricultural productivity.

How It Works

Using state-of-the-art AI and machine learning algorithms, AgriDetect analyzes images of plant leaves to detect early signs of diseases. The system is designed to be user-friendly, allowing farmers to upload leaf images from their mobile devices and receive instant disease predictions, along with recommendations for treatment and prevention.

Why Choose AgriDetect?

  • Accuracy: Powered by advanced AI models trained on a large dataset of plant diseases.
  • Speed: Quick analysis of plant leaf images for immediate action.
  • Ease of Use: Simple interface designed for farmers with no technical background.
  • Cost-Effective: Reduce losses and improve crop yields with early disease detection.
